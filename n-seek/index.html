N-Seek

"Join us for our tribannual Team Restructuring Seminar! Start your Onboarding Journey to become a part of the Team. Build your resume, Meet the [Higher-Ups], Complete your Applicontract, and join our N-Seek Corporeal Family. (Play a Meshtastic LoRa radio based, ARG fueled, scavenger hunt)"

The "N-Seek Onboarding Experience" was a multimedia Alternate Reality Game and Scavenger hunt built around costumes finding costumed characters using open source Meshtastic radio technology. Players would have to complete their "Applicontract" by navigating a dimensionally warped parody of corporate life using hidden websites, voicemail recordings, coded messages, and clues from costumed actors in order to receive their onboarding prize.

This experience was exhibited at the San Francisco Maker Faire Open Sauce in July 2025.

Voted 10th place for "Best in Sauce" out of over 500 exhibitors.
