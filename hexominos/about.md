# Create and Share your own Rulesets
Hexonimos serve as building blocks for creating your very own games. Like a deck of cards or bag of dice, how you play is up to you. Whether it's a small house-rule change to an existing way to play or a whole gamemode with new custom pieces. Players can create new games and share them on the Hexominos Gamemode Forum.


# Make your own Open Source Pieces
Anyone can 3D Print, CNC, Carve, or Cut Out their own Hexominos. This can range from testing out the paper cut out demo Hexominos before buying a full set, to creating and 3D printing your own custom pieces for a unique game mode. All model files are openly shared for players to create their own Hexominos anyway they see fit.

# The Right Way to Play for Anyone
With multiple game modes and endless opportunity to customize, Hexominos are fit for the most competitive or the most casual players. As the board, card, and tabletop gaming market spreads out both in age and internationally, a game that changes to fit the players without spending more time or money for new games provides flexibility and replayability.
