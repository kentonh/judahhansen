# Ominous Obelisk

**User Created:** Sarah Hiland

**Players:** 1–4
**Playtime:** 5–15 Minutes

## Setup

1. Draw one random pattern card and create that pattern as your base.
2. Players roll off for who goes first.

## Play

1. Start your turn by rolling the die for the number of pieces you will add to the obelisk (one to four).
2. Roll the die again for each piece you will stack to determine which Hexominos are added.
3. You may add all of your pieces at once in order to balance and stack precarious additions, but you may not touch or move any pieces already part of the obelisk from previous turns.
4. Play continues until the Obelisk falls.

## Scoring

The player who knocks over the Obelisk is the loser and all other players win.
